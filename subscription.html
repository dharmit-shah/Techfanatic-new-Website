Frequently asked Questions

Who will be designing my projects?

We have another experienced team of designers at the Wavespace who are versatile in different fields of expertise in design, including UX/UI design, branding, SaaS, and mobile applications. When you partner with us, your project will be handled by a team of top-tier designers who have worked on numerous high-profile projects. You will be able to get people who are in the best position to know your unique needs and design according to your brand. Your project will not go to just any designer, but one who will ensure the best, willing to bring in the difference on your project.

How do I know your designs will match my brand and goals?

Absolutely! We understand that collaboration is key to bringing your product vision to life. In case you have an internal development team or an outsourced one, we would easily work with that team and make the implementation proper and smooth. Our files will easily blend into your workflow, and our team will be willing to help you when it comes to the stage of development. This partnership makes sure that your vision of the design stays true, but at the same time, satisfies technical requirements and gives the desired user experience.

Can you work with my developer or product team?

Absolutely! We know that the only way to make your vision of a product materialize is by working together. You may have an in-house development team or external contractors, and our team works hand in hand with them to deliver a smooth implementation. We prepare our design files in such a way that they can easily fit into your workflow, and we are always prepared to assist you at the development stage. Such a partnership can help to achieve the balance between maintaining the design vision and sticking to technical requirements, along with delivering the targeted user experience.

What happens after I subscribe?

Once you subscribe, we immediately start working on your project. First, we’ll have an onboarding session to fully understand your needs, brand, and goals. Once that is done, we give you a schedule, present deliverables, and start working on the design process. You will get updates regularly, and our team is going to be in touch with you so that the project runs smoothly. No matter the size of the tweak or a redesign, our team will make sure you are in the process at each and every stage.

What if I don’t have design knowledge?

You do not have to be a design guru to be able to work with us. We know that not everyone is familiar with the terms in the design industry, which is why we simplify your process. We allow our team time to discuss the process and provide our insights on design decisions, so that you feel secure in the path we are taking. We know that every now and then, you may be unsure, and that is why we are here to clarify your doubts and help you in the process. Just imagine us as your advisor who will help you transform your ideas into reality, even without being a design guru.

What if I’m not happy with the design?

Wavespace aims to surpass your expectations. In case you are not satisfied with the design, we will communicate with you to figure out the reasons and make necessary changes. We believe in continuous collaboration and value your feedback. We make sure that you have lots of chances to share your opinion and make sure that the design will meet your expectations through the process. We will make adjustments till you are satisfied with perfect results. Your happiness is what we are about, and we keep going until we have something that you truly love.

I’ve been burned by vague deliverables before. What exactly do I get?

We know how annoying it is to have vague deliverables. At Wavespace, transparency is key. Each project will allow us to give you a clear, organized plan with all the details of what will be presented to you, such as wireframes, design mockups, a prototype, and the rest. We will ensure that we provide quality work and all the detailed work as per your expectations. At every step, you will be fully aware of what will happen, and we also have communication channels that remain open to make you aware of the developments. No surprises, just clear, actionable design assets that will help move your project forward.

Do you offer meetings?

Yes! We also believe in the openness of communication between us and our clients. So, we offer regular meetings to discuss progress, answer questions, and gather feedback. These meetings make a significant contribution to our collaboration process, as we always have the same direction as you and your vision. It may be via video call, phone call, or meeting face-to-face; whatever the case, in your place, we will ensure that you are kept in the loop and are involved in every process of the project.

Where can I see the task progress?

We use a project management system that allows you to track the progress of your design tasks in real time. You will be able to see a specific dashboard in which you can check milestones, timeline, or accomplished tasks. This will make you relaxed since your project will be running smoothly. You will also be in constant touch with our team as you will be receiving regular updates. We will ensure your involvement and communication during the process of design.

Can I pause or cancel my subscription anytime?

We realize that the requirements of a business may vary, and flexibility matters. You will have the freedom to pause or cancel our subscription at any time. In case you require a pause or changing your subscription according to the necessary needs, we will gladly do it. We want to make sure that with us, it is a pleasant and convenient experience to work. You can take more time or have a scope change, and we will be there for you.

How do I submit design requests?

Submitting design requests is simple and straightforward. When you become a client, you will be able to use our project management platform, where you can put in new design requests, give feedback, and keep track of progress. We are going to align with your wishes and make sure that every request is handled with utmost consideration. We want to ensure the process of communication about your needs and awareness of your project status is as convenient as possible.

I don’t want to be locked into a long-term contract. What are my options?

We know that two years can be a drag, and this is more so when your needs are changing. This is why we have a flexible subscription plan with no long-term contracts. You can adjust your plan or pause your subscription whenever you need. We want to give you the flexibility in choosing what suits you without giving hard and fast terms. Are you searching for a brief design partnership or a long-term collaboration? We have a solution for you, it suits your needs.
