Why Malaysian businesses choose Wavespace

Wavespace has a user-centered approach to designing systemic solutions to the specific problems affecting Malaysia, but within global standards. Our designs focus on improving user experience and delivering measurable business results.

01
Multilingual, multicultural fit

We understand Malaysia’s diverse demographic. By designing multilingual interfaces that support Bahasa Malaysia, English, and Mandarin, we ensure every user, whether Bumiputera, Chinese, or Indian, feels at home, leading to higher user adoption and engagement.

02
Mobile-first focus

With 90% smartphone penetration in Malaysia (DataReportal), we prioritize mobile-first designs that ensure thumb-friendly flows. Our mobile designs are optimized for low-bandwidth connections, making them perfect for Malaysia’s 4G/5G networks and contributing to faster conversion rates.

Privacy and accessibility are paramount in Malaysia. Our designs adhere to PDPA 2024 regulations, ensuring privacy-by-design. We also meet WCAG 2.1 AA standards, promoting accessibility for all, including Persons with Disabilities (PWD), which is vital for creating inclusive user experiences.
