Build your product

UI UX Consulting

Wavespace helps you get clarity before you build. Whether you're shaping a new idea or refining an MVP, our UI UX consulting aligns your goals, your users' needs, and your tech scope.

UX Research

You can’t design for users you don’t understand. We run lean, targeted research to uncover what real users want and what frustrates them. These insights shape smarter products, smoother flows, and better adoption.

Usability Testing

Stop guessing. Start testing. We find out where users get stuck, why, and then, you can fix what matters. From onboarding to dashboards, we help you design a flow that is as easy as breathing.

Wireframing & Prototyping

Before investing on development, test the experience. We take your idea and turn it into wireframes and interactive prototypes that let you validate quick, saving you time, budget and product pains.

UX Audit

If something feels off and you have no idea why, then we dig in. Our UX audit exposes bottlenecks, breaks, and usability problems that affect your product. You will get the usable solutions not the obscure advices.

UI UX Design

Design that isn’t just beautiful, it works. We build intuitive interfaces that guide users, earn trust, and drive action. Every screen is tailored to your product’s purpose and your users’ behavior.

Design System Audit

Scaling? Don’t let a messy design slow you down. We audit and clean up your design system, so your team works faster, your brand stays consistent, and your product feels cohesive.

Visual Design

It is not only about looking good. We create visual experiences that trigger emotion, represent your brand, and make a home for users. It’s the beauty with a purpose, and the performance.
