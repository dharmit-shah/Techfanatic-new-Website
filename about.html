Founder’s
Led Journey
Shahid Miah
2019, When I got my vision

Back in 2014, I had just finished high school. No time to celebrate, I was already thinking, how do I help my family?

Like most kids from a middle-class home, I aimed for engineering. Design? Never crossed my mind. But when my uncle nudged me toward graphic arts, I followed, not out of passion, but because it was something. Somewhere that journey, I stumbled into UI/UX. No mentor, no roadmap. Just late nights, curiosity, and a deep urge to make things make sense. I kept wondering, how can design solve real problems?

Shahid Miah
2019, When I got my vision

That question lit a fire in me. One project led to another & before long, I was designing for bigger companies, across borders, and I finally wanted to work abroad by going. By then, the dream had changed. It wasn’t just about earning or designing for clients anymore.

In 2019, I paused and dug deeper, not into tools, but into why products fail. And from that, Wavespace was born. I never planned to build an agency. I just wanted to solve problems.  That mission hasn’t changed. We don’t show up to impress.

I wanted to understand,
Why do great founders fail, and why do strong products disappear? That one question led me across projects, borders, and eventually, toward something bigger.

We show up to help founders who were once like me, full of vision, short on clarity. We design for outcomes. Because future Unicorns aren’t found, they’re designed. Now I’m here, ready to team up with venture capital, building what’s next, with clarity, speed, and heart.

Wavespace started with design, now we’re building future unicorns. With VC partners and early-stage founders, we combine clarity, code, and capital to launch what’s next.

I didn’t plan to start an agency. I was watching friends & founders fail to turn ideas into usable product.

Whether it’s a mobile app for a startup or a SaaS tool built to grow, we make sure every screen is both good-looking and easy to use.

At Wavespace, we take time to know your brand and your users. From planning to testing, we tweak every part to make smooth and fun user paths. Our skills include UI UX design, web building, branding, and more, but we love turning ideas into designs that work.

What digital design tools and technologies do you use for UI UX design?

For UI/UX design, we use a mix of strong tools that help us make simple yet powerful designs. As a UI UX design company, we use Figma and Sketch for interface design, letting our team work together smoothly. These tools help us adjust and improve designs fast.

To prototype, we use ProtoPie to add interactive features to the design. This makes sure it looks good and works well too. For user testing, we use tools like Maze and Lookback to learn how real users feel about the design. It helps us improve the design so that users can easily use it and want to come back.

We focus on information architecture, setting up the design so it’s easy to move around. With these tools and skills combined, we offer user experience design solutions that boost your brand and keep users engaged.

How long does the entire design process take?

The design process usually takes a few weeks to a couple of months, depending on the project’s size and how complex it is. First, we work closely with you to understand your needs, goals, and brand. This helps us build a strong base for the project. But approximately 4 weeks to 8 weeks we take.

Once we know the direction, we move on to making basic wireframes and prototypes. This stage lets us get feedback early to ensure the design matches your vision. We often run user experience design solutions to test how easy it is to use and make sure the design fits your users.

We are in close contact through the whole process and can quickly change the design according to your thoughts. The final design is user-friendly, effective, and helps your business. At the end, we assure it looks not only stunning but also would be user-friendly and work in harmony with your brand.
